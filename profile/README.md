## MAHI Lab: Machine-and-Human Intelligence Laboratory

Department of Computer Science, BITS Pilani, K K Birla Goa Campus, India  
PI: [Tirtharaj Dash](https://tirtharajdash.github.io)

Our primary aim is to do "AI for Science". We work on: Neurosymbolic AI, Human-in-the-Loop ML, Deep Learning, AI for Molecular Biology, AI for Drug Discovery.

Visit our [group website](https://mahi-group.github.io) for more information and opportunities to work with us!
